Bachelorprojektets titel
	Segmentering af blodårer i angiogrammer vha præ-træning med generative modeller

Nærmere beskrivelse
We will use a generative model to generate a simple 2D visual approximation of a heart's blood vessels as seen on angiograms. We will use this model to generate synthetic data.
To this data we will add noise simulating the noise that will be on actual angiograms.
We will then train 2 machine learning models, e.g. a Unet and Visual Transformer, on the synthetic data.
These models should perform semantic segmentation of the blood vessels in the noisy synthetic data.
We wish for the semantic segmentation to resemble the synthetic data without noise as closely as possible.
Afterwards we will train the 2 models on actual angiograms. Data is available.
Lastly we will compare the 2 models.
